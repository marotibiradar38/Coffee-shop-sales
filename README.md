# **☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Jan–Jun) —  Data Analytics Project** 

This repository contains a fully interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel**, analyzing six months of sales, customer behavior, and product performance.
The dashboard converts raw transactional data into clear, actionable insights for decision-making across **operations, marketing, and inventory management**. 

---
  
## 📂 **Project Overview**   

This project explores key sales metrics from **January to June**, highlighting customer footfall, sales patterns, product category contributions, order size preferences, and top-selling items.
The goal is to demonstrate real-world **data cleaning, visualization, and business analytics** skills within  Excel. 
   
--- 

## 🚀 **Features of the Dashboard**  

✔ *Interactive slicers* for Month, Product Category, Store Location, Size, and Product Types 
✔ *Dynamic charts and KPIs* connected through Excel tables
✔ *Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts* for automatic updates 
✔ *Auto-refresh summaries* to ensure clean and consistent reporting
✔ *Professional layout with clear design and minimalistic theme.

---

## 📈 **Insights Generated**
 
### **1. Hourly Order Trends (Operations)**

* Orders peak between **8 AM – 12 PM**, especially at 10 AM.
* *Recommendation:* Increase staffing during peak hours and introduce off-peak promotions.

### **2. Category Contribution (Product Mix)**

* Coffee contributes **39%** of total sales.
* Packaged Chocolate and Bakery items show strong cross-selling potential.
* *Recommendation:* Promote high-margin non-coffee items near billing counters.

### **3. Size Distribution (Pricing Strategy)**

* Regular and Large sizes constitute **60%+** of total orders.
* A significant portion fall under “Undefined.”
* *Recommendation:* Review data entry/categorization to reduce undefined entries.

### **4. Top 5 Product Types (Marketing & Inventory)**

* Products like **Barista Espresso** and **Brewed Chai** lead the sales chart.
* *Recommendation:* Ensure high availability and consider targeted combo offers.
